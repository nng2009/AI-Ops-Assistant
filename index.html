About Smart AIOps Assistant

Smart AIOps Assistant helps IT and DevOps teams manage complex systems by automating log analysis. Instead of manually scanning thousands of log lines, users can rely on AI‑driven insights to identify failures, understand why they occurred, and take corrective action faster.

Features

Upload and analyze system log files

AI identifies patterns and flags issues automatically.

AI‑based root cause explanation

Get simple explanations of why issues occurred.

Actionable recommendations to fix issues

Receive step-by-step solutions to resolve problems.

Interactive assistant for system insights

Ask questions and get AI-powered responses.
